# Job-fit score (JFS) — out of scope

This bundle ships **ILS estimate** triage only (`docs/reference_ils_scoring_model.md`).

**Job-fit score (JFS)** is a separate manual research pass (happiness / tailoring depth, C1–C7 dimensions). It is intentionally **not** automated here.

Use your own research workflow after triage marks a row `apply` and ILS clears your floor.
